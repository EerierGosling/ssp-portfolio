# ssp portfolio

This is my portfolio for my Senior Spring Project! At Buckingham Browne & Nichols School, all seniors spend the spring trimester doing activities of their choosing for 40+ hours a week to fulfill a set of pillars: Intellectual Engagement, Service Learning, New Experience, and Wellness.


The activities I chose are:


Intellectual Engagement: [Advanced Topics in Computer Science](https://ssp.eerier.dev/atcs)


Service Learning: [BCYF Coding Club](https://ssp.eerier.dev/bcyf)


New Experience: [Breads & Spreads](https://ssp.eerier.dev/breads-and-spreads)


Wellness: [Sailing](https://ssp.eerier.dev/sailing)


Remaining Hours: [Beyond our Echo Chambers: Understanding the Other](https://ssp.eerier.dev/echo-chambers) and [Book Club](https://ssp.eerier.dev/book-club)


We also have to read a book! I chose to read [1984 by George Orwell](https://ssp.eerier.dev/1984). You can also find my [mid-SSP reflection](https://ssp.eerier.dev/midpoint-reflection) and [final SSP reflection](https://ssp.eerier.dev/final-reflection) on this website.

The carousel of pictures on the main page is a mix of pictures from both my SSP activities and things I did outside of SSP!)_
